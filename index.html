Your Child's 5-Day Adventure

A structured programme that guides children through five days of creativity, adventure, and skill building.

Day 1

Kick-Off & Creativity

Camp begins with introductions, ice breakers, and tribe formations to build friendships. Children dive into a hands-on Art Jam workshop to express themselves through colour and creativity.

Day 2

Storytelling Through Comics

Campers learn the basics of storytelling and visual expression, then experiment with illustration and storyboarding to create their very own comic.

Day 3

Adventure & Resilience

A full day outdoors guided by expert facilitators. Children take on team-based challenges that build courage, problem-solving, and resilience in action.

Day 4

Curiosity, Discovery & Community

The world of science comes alive through engaging experiments and projects. Campers work in teams to prepare their own science showcase, with extended time in the evening for preparation for their final showcase.

Day 5

Shine & Celebrate

Campers refine their presentations through a public speaking workshop, finalise their projects, and present at the Edventure Science Fair 2025. Families and friends are invited to share in this proud moment.

About Us

Edventure Camp was created by proven educators for curious, adventure-seeking students. Backed by the same team behind Mind Stretcher's highly rated holiday programmes, our camp combines years of experience with a strong belief in every child's potential.

Our mission is clear: to provide a safe, nurturing, and fulfilling platform where children can learn, explore, and grow. With a carefully designed programme, close guidance, and trusted supervision, we help children maximise their time, discover new strengths, and build confidence that lasts well beyond the camp.

FAQ

Children gain life skills in creativity, resilience, and teamwork while enjoying a unique blend of learning and fun, and making new friends.

The camp is open to children born between 2013 and 2018 who have a basic understanding of English, as activities will be conducted in English.

Camps run weekly from Monday to Friday, 24 November to 19 December 2025, at Mind Stretcher East Campus (NTUC Income @ Tampines Junction).

Each day includes arrival, structured activities, and mealtimes, with a mix of indoor and outdoor hands-on experiences, culminating in a showcase at the end of the week.

Nutritious hot lunches and snacks are provided daily, and parents can inform us of any allergies or medical needs during registration.

At registration, two-way bus transport option is available daily between Mind Sparks Toa Payoh and East Campus, departing at 8am and returning around 7pm depending on traffic. There will be a transport surcharge of $40 for the entire week.

Simply click on the registration link and complete the form to secure your child's place.

We offer a 50% refund for cancellations made 30 days before camp, 25% for cancellations 14–29 days before, and no refund within 14 days. If we cancel the camp, you will receive a full refund.
